ReMedic Hospital was established in 1986.

ReMedic is furnished with excellent infrastructure and equipped with the best doctors and research facilities for several diseases.
ReMedic as a leading training institute for medical students attracts medical students from all over India.
Patients from all over India as well as from abroad come to this government hospital at the times of critical illness.
Patients below the poverty line also can afford this hospital.
In 2014, the India Today Group declared ReMedic Hospital as one of the top hospitals in Guntur as well as in AP.
